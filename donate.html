Solidarity is an element of human association that emphasizes the cohesive social bond that holds a group together, which is valued and understood by all group members.There are different motives for solidarity. For some, affection and shared norms and beliefs are motives, while for others, rational choice and self-interest are drivers. The concepts of trust, altruism, and reciprocity are grouped under individual acts, as they are instituted from an individual standpoint. Finally remember that every cent you paid may save a human.
